Hello lovely people!

Before jumping into the What and How I'd like to start with the Why. To be completely honest, my skills (What) and approaches (How) are quite interchangeable and there are dozens of equally qualified people out there. So what is it that differentiates me from these other folks in the wild?

In actual fact I do love my job! Currently, I work for Jung von Matt by leading a team of developers and being a thoughtful technical director. Some people might say JvM is a company full of egomaniacs and creative freaks. Others might think employees at JvM do work round the clock while creating chaos and tend to live in the agency offices.

Actually, for a small minority of individuals this may be true. But do you know what? The people I got to know are cooperative, empathetic and most of all passionate about what they do in their jobs. Be it generating creative ideas non-stop, managing clients and projects with heart and soul or developing state-of-the-art web applications – people at JvM really reach for excellent results and "remaining dissatisfied" – one of JvM’s core principles – is not a mere cliche!

My career stopovers at IBM were different in terms of work approaches, used technologies and project goals. But there too the people made the difference! An "IBMer" is proud to work at Big Blue and I've met people from all over the world with various backgrounds in terms of culture and profession. Putting so much effort into high-end tech always fascinated me and my colleagues were brilliant thinkers when it came to complex systems and architectures.

7 years IBM 7 years JvM

Probably, it has been these facts which made it difficult for me to decide whether to work in the big corporate or in the creative world. Both domains have their advantages and disadvantages - for sure. In the end, I learned so many things: Professionally and personally.

But now for me is the time for a change. To make a change. To create something meaningful. I really want to get to know new people, new ways of working and new approaches of building something with real value!

If you would like to work with me, let's meet, let's discuss, let's collaborate and make business together.
